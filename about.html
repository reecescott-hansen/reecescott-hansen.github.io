My Story

Growing up in New York City, I was always surrounded by the world of finance — and a lot of that came from watching my dad. He works in finance and has always been my biggest inspiration. Seeing how he understood the way businesses and markets work made me want to learn the same things. That curiosity turned into a genuine passion.

At the start of my freshman year at Columbia Grammar and Preparatory School, I decided to turn that passion into something real. I joined a Web Design class, learned HTML and CSS from scratch, and used those skills to build this website — a place where I could track stocks, manage a mock portfolio, and share what I am learning about investing as I go.

What started as a class project has grown into something I genuinely care about. I have built a 14-stock mock portfolio, added a live market tracker powered by real financial data, and launched a freelance web design service. Every part of this site was built by me — no templates, no shortcuts.

I do not know exactly where finance will take me yet — whether that is investment banking, running my own fund, or building a business from the ground up. But I know that the best way to figure that out is to start now, stay curious, and keep building.
