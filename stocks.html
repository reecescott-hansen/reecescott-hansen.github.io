S&P 500 (SPY)

Type: ETF / Market Index

Rating: ★★★★★

What it is: The S&P 500 tracks 500 of the largest U.S. companies and provides a broad picture of the overall stock market.

Why I chose it: The S&P 500 is one of the best ways to understand the overall U.S. market at a glance.

Strength: Diversified across many industries, reducing risk compared to individual stocks.

Risk: If the overall market falls, the S&P 500 falls too — there is no protection from broad market downturns.

My View: The S&P 500 is one of the most important things to study because it reflects the entire market.
