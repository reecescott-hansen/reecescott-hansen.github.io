Finance Through a Student Lens

Welcome to my finance portfolio website. I created this site to combine my interest in investing and economics with the web design skills I am learning in school.

About Me

I am a class of 2028 high school student from New York City interested in finance, business, and economics. I attend Columbia Grammar and Preparatory School on the Upper West Side. At the start of my freshman year, I was very interested in both the finance world and coding, so I decided to join a Web Design class.

In Web Design, I learned HTML and CSS and decided to use those skills to build this website. Here I explore stock analysis, track my mock portfolio, and share ideas as I build my understanding of investing. Ever since I was little I have wanted to understand how our world works — and my dad, who works in finance, is the reason I ultimately decided to pursue it.

Latest Update

This month, I expanded my mock portfolio, added a stock analysis page with ratings and research for each company, and launched a new Hire Me page where I offer freelance web design services to local businesses. My goal is to keep growing this site as I learn more about both finance and web development.
