Latest Update

Over the past few weeks, I have continued building my finance website and expanding it into a larger project. What started as a simple homepage has now grown into a multi-page website with sections for stock analysis, my mock portfolio, and project updates. One of my main goals has been to make the website look more professional while also improving my HTML and CSS skills.

Recently, I added a market snapshot bar at the top of the site to make the homepage feel more like a real finance website. Even though the data is not live, I included it to show the major indexes I am following and to make the site more visually interesting. I also added logos for different companies on the stock analysis page, which helped make each section clearer and more organized.

Another update I made was expanding my mock portfolio. At first, I only had a few stocks, but now I have added more companies from different industries such as technology, finance, consumer brands, entertainment, and aerospace. This helped me make the portfolio more balanced and also gave me more companies to research and compare.

As I keep working on this project, I want to continue improving both the design and the content. My next steps are to update my stock analysis more often, improve the layout of some pages, and keep learning how finance, investing, and markets connect to the companies I am studying.
